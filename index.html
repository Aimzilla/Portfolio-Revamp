A little about me...

My journey into tech started with an undergraduate degree in Sociology and Psychology, as well as a Master's degree in Business. After 20 years of working in various roles in the insurance industry, I chucked it all, and joined the Peace Corps where I served in Namibia. While there, I became aware of just how much technology was used around the world, and realized what a valuable tool to teach finances, health, and language.

It was then that I decided that upon my return to the U.S. that I wanted to go into tech. After taking several courses online and attending a coding bootcamp, I decided that I am most interested in website development and front-end code of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I hope to use my new skills coupled with my pre-existing skillset to work for non-profits and marginalized communities writing clean, elegant, and accessible code.
